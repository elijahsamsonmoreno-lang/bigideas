Starting Point
We built this short diagnostic for people carrying real work in and alongside Indian Country who are trying to make sense of a crowded landscape of needs, opportunities, and partners. In three questions, it gives you a clearer picture of where your project sits today and what kind of support would actually help.

You can use these results with or without us. Our goal is to offer a small public good: a sharper starting point for your own planning, conversations with leadership, or talks with potential partners.

Where do you sit?

Pick the option that best matches your role or the client you're thinking about.

Where do you most need support right now?

Not everything at once—where would change unlock the most downstream impact?

What's the friction right now?

These are all common. Pick the one that feels closest today.
